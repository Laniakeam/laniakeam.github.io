Privacy Policy for Stack Rush

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Stack Rush ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you play our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

Stack Rush is designed to be a privacy-friendly game.

  • Personal Data: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to play the game.
  • Automatically Collected Information: When you access the game, we may automatically collect certain non-personal information, including:
    • Device type and model.
    • Operating system version.
    • Game performance data (e.g., high scores, crash logs).

2. Third-Party Services

  • Huawei Ads / Advertising Partners: These services may collect your device's advertising ID to show personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Google Play Services / Apple Game Center: If you sign in to these services, they may collect your game progress and achievements according to their own privacy policies.

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve and optimize the game experience.
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends and activities.
  • Fix technical issues and bugs.
  • Provide advertising (if applicable).

4. Data Retention

We do not store your personal data on our servers. Game progress is typically stored locally on your device unless synced with third-party services.

5. Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect any personal identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

6. Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting information, but no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
