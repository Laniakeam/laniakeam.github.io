1. Introduction

PassCheck – Password Security (referred to as "the Application" or "the Service") is developed by Reny R. Medina Martinez ("We", "Us", or "Our").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your sensitive data remains under your exclusive control.

2. Core Privacy Principle: Zero-Server Architecture

Unlike traditional cloud-based managers, PassCheck operates on a Zero-Server model:

  • Local Encryption: All passwords, 2FA tokens, and credentials are encrypted using AES-256 and stored locally.
  • No Cloud Transmission: We do not maintain external servers and cannot access your data.
  • Exclusive Access: You are the sole owner of your decryption keys.

3. Types of Data Collected

A. Personal Data

The app may request access to:

  • Biometric authentication
  • Camera access for QR/OCR
  • Storage access for backups and PDFs

B. Usage Data

Anonymous diagnostic and performance data may be collected automatically and is not used to personally identify users.

4. Third-Party Services & Local AI

  • Huawei Mobile Services (HMS)
  • Google ML Kit for local OCR processing

No sensitive information leaves the device.

5. Advertising (Huawei Ads)

The Application may display advertisements provided by Huawei Ads to support development.

Huawei Ads may collect anonymized identifiers, approximate location and usage data to improve ad relevance.

6. Use of Your Data

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Improve performance and security
  • Perform local password analysis

We do NOT sell or share personal data.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data is deleted when the app is uninstalled.
  • Users are responsible for securing backups.

8. Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for users under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Legal Compliance (GDPR / PIPL)

This policy complies with global data protection regulations, including GDPR and PIPL.

Users maintain full control over their data.
